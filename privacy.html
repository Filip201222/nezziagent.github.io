Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2025

Nezzi Agent ("we", "our", "us") operates a social media management platform that integrates with TikTok's API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

By using Nezzi Agent, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information we receive through TikTok's API when you authorize our application:

  • TikTok Profile Information: Your TikTok username, display name, profile picture, and unique user ID (via user.info.basic and user.info.profile scopes)
  • Account Statistics: Follower count, following count, and total likes (via user.info.stats scope)
  • Video Data: Your video list, video titles, descriptions, and metadata (via video.list scope)
  • Uploaded Content: Videos you choose to upload and publish through our platform (via video.upload scope)
  • Usage Data: Log data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account session
  • To display your TikTok profile and statistics on your dashboard
  • To schedule and publish video content to TikTok on your behalf
  • To display your existing TikTok video library
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • To respond to support requests and communicate with you

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • TikTok: We interact with TikTok's official API on your behalf to perform actions you authorize
  • Service Providers: Trusted infrastructure providers (hosting, analytics) contractually bound to protect your data
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety of our users

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. If you disconnect your TikTok account or request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.

5. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure storage of access tokens
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Minimal data collection — we only request permissions we actually use

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Revoke TikTok authorization at any time through TikTok's app settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nezziagent@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Nezzi Agent is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
